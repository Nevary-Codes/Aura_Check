What is Aura Check?

Aura Check is not a quiz; it is an AI-driven analysis system. Predictions are based on structured survey responses processed through advanced algorithms.

Aura Check is designed as a demonstration of how machine learning can be used to analyze mental health indicators in a responsible, privacy-first way.
